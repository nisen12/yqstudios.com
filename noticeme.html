Noticeme - Mobile App

Visual Design, Content Design, Web Design, App Design, Visual Composition
Personal Project, 2025

Noticeme is a lifestyle finance app that blends personal expense management with social interaction. It helps users track spending, manage cards and bills, and share costs effortlessly with friends — making financial management more visual, social, and intuitive.

Problem Discovery & Design Solution
Bridging the gap between finance and friendship — designing a simple, social, and intuitive way to manage money.
Design Process Overview
Noticeme was built through a human-centered process that transforms research insights into clear, social, and intuitive design. Each step—from user interviews to visual refinement—focused on making financial interactions effortless, friendly, and emotionally engaging.
Interaction Design & Motion
Every interaction detail was meticulously crafted. Card swiping effects let users easily browse different bank cards, while pop-up notifications present important information non-intrusively. Haptic feedback enhances user confidence in their actions.
Smart Calendar View
A visual timeline that brings order to payments and planning.
Cards & Statistics
Visualizing spending patterns through clarity, color, and motion — transforming raw numbers into meaningful insights. Every chart and interaction is designed to make complex data feel intuitive, helping users recognize habits, spot trends, and stay in control of their financial rhythm.
Desktop & iPad Experience
The iPad and desktop versions extend Noticeme beyond mobile, offering a wider, more immersive workspace. The responsive grid layout adapts naturally, keeping key actions and insights always within reach. Users can view charts, transactions, and reminders side by side, maintaining flow without losing focus. Consistent motion, color balance, and typography unify the experience across every device.

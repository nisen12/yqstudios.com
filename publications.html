Mom's Garden How we nurture, bloom, and evolve. Stories and reflections on the tenderness of nature and maternal care. "In the garden, each flower, tenderly cultivated by my mother's hands, narrates a story of growth and resilience. The cyanotypes within these pages capture more than the visual splendor of the flora; they embody the enduring gentleness of her nurturing spirit. The fabric that binds this book mirrors her influence—soft yet all-encompassing. The selection of materials mirrors the comforting and protective essence of maternal care, reflecting the warmth and gentleness she bestows upon all she nurtures." Dec. 2021

Cyanotypes on organic cotton fabric

24 pp. 10 img

Recycled Cotton fabric

Reconstructed Dictionaries How language shapes us, our culture, and our understanding of the world. Stories and reflections on the profound impact of literacy and education. "Once the cornerstone of learning in every Chinese home, the Xinhua Dictionary has not only chronicled words but also the ascent of literacy across generations. Today, it stands as a testament to the power of education to transform societies. This project reimagines the dictionary, preserving its essence through traditional papermaking techniques, transforming each page into a symbol of knowledge’s endurance beyond the physical bounds of the book.This artistic re-envisioning of the Xinhua Dictionary through papermaking explores the continuing relevance of traditional educational tools in a digital age, celebrating both historical achievements in Chinese literacy and the ongoing journey of learning and remembering." Oct. 2021

Handmade Paper 450g/m2

45cm x 70cm

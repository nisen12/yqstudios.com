Gallery Exhibition Biography

New York City-based Quan Yuan specializes in still-life photography, having shot for corporate and retail clients in the U.S. and overseas. Her background in managing entire workflows, from set building to post-production to graphic design, gives her a special understanding of her clients’ marketing and branding needs. The recent recipient of a Master’s in Digital Photography from New York’s prestigious School of Visual Arts, Yuan is also active as a fine-art photographer. Her most recent project, Paper Dream uses sets built of paper to illustrate her dream life, exploring how the unconscious mind influences our personalities and behavior.

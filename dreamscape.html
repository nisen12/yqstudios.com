Dreamscape - Mobile App

Narrative-Based Visual Exploration, Experiential Visual Inquiry, Visual Language Development

Tools: Figma · Adobe Suite · ChatGPT · Claude Code · DeepSeek
Personal Project, 2025

Dreamscape is an experimental visual project that reimagines how individuals engage with their subconscious mind. Rather than functioning as a conventional journaling tool, the project explores dreams as emotional and aesthetic phenomena, emphasizing the visual interpretation of internal states.

Created as an emotion-first system, Dreamscape transforms subconscious reflections into interactive visual experiences, using abstracted forms, color systems, and compositional structures to represent the emotional resonance of dreams. The project situates itself within contemporary visual research, proposing a new framework for translating psychological perception into artistic expression.

Pain Points & Solutions
Understanding user frustrations and transforming them into intuitive emotional experiences.
Home Interface — Emotion-Based Entry
A calm, circular palette invites users to start with feeling before words — turning dream journaling into a mindful ritual.
Design Process & Product Framework
A human-centered workflow that connects research, emotion, and interaction — shaping a seamless system from insight to interface.
Visual Design
Where color, balance, and motion work together to evoke calmness and emotional depth — shaping a system that feels intuitive, cohesive, and human.
Add Entry — Multimodal Input
Flexible journaling that lets users record dreams anytime through voice, text, or image.
Calendar View — Emotion Pattern Visualization
Tracking emotions through weekly flow — where color and motion reveal the rhythm of your inner world.
Insights & Dream Library
From motion to meaning — transforming dreams into visual patterns, insights, and personal stories.
Reflection & Takeaways
Designing for emotion taught me that empathy and simplicity can turn fleeting moments into meaningful experiences.

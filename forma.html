Forma - Wechat Miniprogram

Visual Design · Brand Identity · UI/UX Design · Interaction Design · Generative Design (AI Integration) · Coding · Research & Strategy

Tools: Figma · Adobe Suite · ChatGPT · Claude Code · DeepSeek
Personal Project, 2025

Forma is a WeChat miniprogram designed for an art education center that connects students with creative mentors and professional guidance. The platform simplifies how students explore disciplines, access courses, and communicate with instructors — all within a visually cohesive environment. It transforms the learning process into an intuitive and inspiring experience by combining structured navigation with bold, expressive design.

Developed through user research, competitive analysis, and iterative design, Forma reflects the mindset of creative learners who value clarity, aesthetics, and connection. Every element — from the color system to the mentor cards — is crafted to help users navigate complex information effortlessly while feeling visually engaged and emotionally supported.

Understanding the Users & Design Intent
Exploring the needs behind Forma’s audience and defining the creative direction that shaped its user-centered experience.
Visual System Overview
A system of colors, rhythm, and structure — shaping Forma's visual identity.
Visual Design
Focuses on being clear, bold, and easy to follow. Colors are used not only to decorate but to organize content — each bright block represents a function and brings energy to the interface. The gray background softens the contrast and keeps the layout comfortable to read. The goal is to make the system feel creative, modern, and visually balanced.
Specialties Page
This page helps users understand available study directions at a glance — from Photography and Fashion design and Fashion management — making exploration intuitive and visually engaging.
Mentor Page
The Mentor page introduces the professional instructors and creative advisors of the platform. Each mentor is displayed through a clean, card-based layout that highlights their photo, specialty, and academic background. This structure allows users to easily browse and identify the most relevant mentors for their goals.
Service Page
The Service page presents the range of educational and consulting services offered by the platform. It is divided into two main sections — Full-Service Packages and Individual Options — allowing users to explore the complete process or choose specific support based on their needs.
About Page
It introduces the organization's background, core philosophy, and approach to art education. The design maintains a clean and balanced layout, allowing users to easily read through brand information and understand Forma's values.

A collapsible section at the bottom reveals the platform's contact details, including WeChat, Xiaohongshu, and phone information, making it easy for users to connect directly.
